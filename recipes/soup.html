Light Soup

Description

Most popular soup in Ghana, popularly served with Fufu

Ingredients

  • Chilli Pepper
  • Powdered pepper
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Garden eggs
  • Fish or Meat
  • Seasoning
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Boil the fresh tomato, pepper and onion.
  2. Properly wash your meat or fish.
  3. On low heat, cook your fish and add some diced onions, powdered pepper and salt to taste
  4. Blend your cooked vegetables.
  5. Add your blended vegetables to your fish or meat and allow to cook for 2 mins<./li>
  6. Add your preferred quantity of water to dilute and allow to boil.
  7. Then add your tomato paste, after a while add your seasoning.
  8. Remove the soup from fire after 30 mins.
