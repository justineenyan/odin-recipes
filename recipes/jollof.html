Jollof Rice

Description

Spiced Jollof rice, can be served with shito

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • vegetables(Pepper, Tomatoes, onion, ginger and garlic)
  • Pepper
  • Powdered pepper
  • Beef
  • Maggi cube
  • Bay leaves
  • White pepper
  • vegetable oil
  1. Pour your preferred quantity of oil into a saucepan and heat for 5 mins.
  2. Add your diced onions and beef and stir until golden brown
  3. Blend and add your fresh tomato, ginger, garlic and pepper and sir for 8 mins
  4. Add your tomato paste and powdered pepper and stir till evenly mixed
  5. Add your maggi cube, bay leaves and white pepper, stir till your sauce is well cooked
  6. Then, add your washed rice and stir till evenly mixed for 5 mins, add salt to taste
  7. Add a good quantity of water and allow to cook
  8. Between 15 mins interval stir your rice and adjust the heat till your rice is properly cooked.
