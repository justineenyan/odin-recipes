Stir-fried Noodles

Description

Spicy Stir-fried noodles particularly loved by Ghanaian teenagers

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of indomie hungry man size
  • Sausage
  • 2 eggs
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Spring Onions
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable oil
  • Powdered pepper
  • Oyster sauce

Steps

  1. boil the indomie noodle for 8 mins
  2. Chop and dice your vegetables
  3. Mince your sausage into tiny pieces
  4. Using any saucepan, pour a satisfied quantity of your Vegetable oil into the pan
  5. heat your oil for 5min and fry your diced sausages
  6. Then, add your diced onion and garlic and egg
  7. Add the rest of the rest of your vegetables, stir till slightly cooked
  8. Add your drained noodles and stir till evenly mixed
  9. Add a preferred quantity of your Oyster sauce and continue to stir
  10. Then add your pepper and indomie spices respectively and continue to stir
