Fried Potato and Chicken

Description

Fried Potato with spicy chicken, served preferably in the afternoon

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes
  • white pepper
  • Chicken breast
  • Powdered pepper
  • Rosemary leaves
  • Powdered pepper
  • Garlic, maggi cube
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Chop your potatoes into small chunks
  2. Heat your oil for 5 mins
  3. Then, fry your potatoes until golden brown
  4. Wash and cut your chicken into small chunks
  5. In a mixing bowl, add your chicken first
  6. Then proceed to add your white pepper, red pepper, grinded rosemary and garlic
  7. Evenly mix them together and then add the maggi cube to it
  8. In a heated oil, fry your chicken until golden brown
  9. Can be served with hibiscus drink
